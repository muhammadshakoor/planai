# PlanAI — User Guide

PlanAI is an AI-powered planning tool. You create a workspace (e.g. "Exam Preparation"), then ask the AI assistant to build a structured plan for you as a tree of topics and tasks. Each task can hold notes and be marked as complete.

---

## 1. Create Your Account

1. Open the app in your browser
2. Click **"Register"**
3. Enter your email and a password
4. Click **"Create Account"** — you are logged in immediately

> Next time, use **"Sign In"** with the same email and password.

---

## 2. Create a Workspace

A workspace is your top-level project (e.g. "Exam Preparation", "Product Launch", "Ramadan Goals").

1. In the left sidebar, click the **+** button next to **WORKSPACES**
2. Type the workspace name (e.g. `Exam Preparation`)
3. Press **Enter** or click **Add**

The workspace opens and the AI Assistant appears on the right.

---

## 3. Use the AI Assistant to Build Your Plan

The AI assistant understands plain commands. Type in the chat box and press **Enter** or click the send button.

### Generate the top-level plan

```
Generate top level plan
```

The AI creates the main categories for your workspace (e.g. Math, Science, English, History).

### Go deeper — generate subtopics

```
Generate subtopics for Math
```

The AI adds topics under Math (e.g. Algebra, Geometry, Calculus).

### Go even deeper

```
Generate subtopics for Algebra
```

The AI adds tasks under Algebra (e.g. Understand Variables, Solve Equations, Practice Problems).

### Add a specific node manually via AI

```
Add node: Review Chapter 5 under Algebra
```

### Example full conversation

| You type | AI does |
|---|---|
| `Generate top level plan` | Creates: Math, Science, English, History |
| `Generate subtopics for Math` | Creates: Algebra, Geometry, Calculus under Math |
| `Generate subtopics for Algebra` | Creates: Variables, Equations, Order of Operations under Algebra |
| `Add node: Past Papers under Math` | Adds Past Papers under Math |

---

## 4. Navigate the Plan Tree

The tree appears in the middle panel.

- Click **▶** next to a node to **expand or collapse** its children
- Click on any node title to **open it** and see its details on the right
- Click **✕** on a node to **delete** it (and all its children)

---

## 5. Work on a Task (Execution Node)

Click any node in the tree to open it on the right side.

1. **Write notes** in the text area — add details, links, or anything relevant
2. Click **"Save Notes"** to save your notes
3. When finished, click **"✓ Mark Complete"**

Completed tasks show a **strikethrough** and a ✓ checkmark in the tree.

---

## 6. Manage Workspaces

- You can have **multiple workspaces** — one per project or goal
- Switch between them by clicking their name in the left sidebar
- Delete a workspace by clicking the **trash icon** (🗑) that appears next to its name

---

## 7. Sign Out

Click **"Sign Out"** at the bottom of the left sidebar.

---

## Quick Reference — AI Commands

| Command | What it does |
|---|---|
| `Generate top level plan` | Creates main categories |
| `Generate subtopics for [Name]` | Creates children under that node |
| `Add node: [Title] under [Parent]` | Adds one specific node |

---

## Tips

- Start with **"Generate top level plan"** for every new workspace
- Keep drilling down with **"Generate subtopics for..."** until you reach small, actionable tasks
- Use the **notes field** to store study material, links, or progress notes
- Mark tasks complete as you go to track your progress visually
